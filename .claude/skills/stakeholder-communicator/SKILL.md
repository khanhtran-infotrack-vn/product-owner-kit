## Best Practices

### Audience Adaptation
- **Executives**: Focus on business impact, metrics, ROI
- **Technical Teams**: Include architecture, dependencies, technical details
- **Customers**: Emphasize benefits, use cases, value delivered
- **Sales/Marketing**: Highlight competitive advantages, customer wins

### Communication Principles
1. **Lead with Impact**: Start with business value, not features
2. **Use Metrics**: Quantify improvements and results
3. **Be Transparent**: Include challenges and how they're addressed
4. **Action-Oriented**: Clear next steps and ownership
5. **Visual**: Use diagrams, charts, screenshots where helpful
